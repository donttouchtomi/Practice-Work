SOLANI IMANI ROWE aka SZA

Solani Imani Rowe aka as SZA is a popular artist who has collaborated with different big artists like Kendrick Lamar, Drake, Kali Uchis, Cardi B etc. Her first studio album 'Z' was released 1999 then she went on to release another album 'Ctrl' where she featured artistes like Travis Scott, Kendrick Lamar she also sang in the Black Panther movie 'Wakanda Forever' soundtrack where she featured Kendrick Lamar on the song ' All the stars' after that she went on to release the deluxe of her last album. SZA also featured Doja Cat on her most played and streamed single titled 'Kiss me more' which won her, her first Grammy awards the song was also nominated for countless awards. After that she went on to release her fourth studio album called 'SOS' which also got so many recognitions like the grammy awards and the likes.
